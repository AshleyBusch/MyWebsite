Music Has Completely Changed My Life

As you can tell, I'm a total music geek. I love everything about it. Music has
always been a universal language. In my opinion, it conveys emotions/feelings
better than anything else. There are so many different kinds of music too, from
hip-hop to classical to heavy metal. There's a type of music for everyone!

Now, for me, I only like certain types of music. As far as listening to/singing
songs goes, I like a LOT of current pop and some classic rock. I also have a
soft spot for emotional ballads (songs with really powerful messages). However, I
do NOT like country or rap at all, or at least any current music in those genres.
There's one category that could go either way for me: musicals. For example, I
absolutely LOVE The Greatest Showman, but I can't stand Hamilton.
(Don't get me started on Disney movies because I can tell you right now that you will
hear WAY more than you want to know about how much I love them.) Anyways, those are
the types of music that I personally enjoy listening to.

Now let's talk about my other music obession: band. I've been in band for a few
years now, which doesn't seem like a long time, but it's been the best few years of
my life. I'm currently in my high school's(Licking Heights) wind ensemble, which is
our top band. Oh, I almost forgot to tell you what I play! I play flute and piccolo,
which has been the best and worst decision I've ever made.
