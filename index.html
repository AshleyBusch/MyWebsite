Hello everyone! If you're on this website it's probably because you like music. Well, that makes two of us. On this website, I'll be talking all
about music. The aspects will range from talking about what kind of music you like, to how it can benefit your mental health. So sit back, and enjoy
the ride!

Why I Made This Website

The main reason I made this website is because I love music. It's always been a huge part of my life, and I wouldn't give it up
for anything. I wanted to make a website where I could talk about a topic that I knew a lot about. For me, that's obviously music.
I want to show people how they can enjoy music, or use it as a tool to help them through their everyday lives. People can learn a lot more
from music than they think.

My goal with this website is to give people information that they wouldn't normally find anywhere else. I want them to gain some sort of helpful knowledge
from the content on here. Music is a somewhat difficult concept for some people to grasp, especially if they aren't good with emotions. That's what I want to
help people understand: that music conveys emotions that we don't always understand by themselves. That's what I'm trying to accomplish with this website.
