Hi. My name is Ashley Busch, and I love music.

Hello everyone! If you're on this website it's probably because you like music. Well, that makes two of us. On this website, I'll be talking all about music. The aspects will range from talking about what kind of music you like, to how it can benefit your mental health. So sit back, and enjoy the ride!

Why I Made This Website

The main reason I made this website is because I love music. It's always been a huge part of my life, and I wouldn't give it up for anything. I wanted to make a website where I could talk about a topic that I knew a lot about. For me, that's obviously music. I want to show people how they can enjoy music, or use it as a tool to help them through their everyday lives. People can learn a lot more from music than they think.

My goal with this website is to give people information that they wouldn't normally find anywhere else. I want them to gain some sort of helpful knowledge from the content on here. Music is a somewhat difficult concept for some people to grasp, especially if they aren't good with emotions. That's what I want to help people understand: that music conveys emotions that we don't always understand by themselves. That's what I'm trying to accomplish with this website.

Everything that I write on this website will be based on my experience and experiences of tother people. I feel like hearing about a shared experience can sometimes be more effective than stating plain facts. After all, facts can't explain emotions. People get a feeling of liberation when they understand something, and emotions are a very difficult thing to try to understand (I don't even understand my own most of the time). That won't stop from finding ways to try to understand them. Music is one of the best ways to understand yourself, and that's why I love it so much. I hope I can help other people understand and discover themselves as well.
